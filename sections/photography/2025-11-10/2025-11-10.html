Full spectrum Venice

2025-11-10

I took these photos in Venice and Burano. The BW ones are taken with a 720nm ir pass filter, while the ones in color are taken in full spectrum without any filter, so not just in visible light.

An interesting feature of "full spectrum" is that it makes water surfaces and sunset skies faintly glow in a greener hue compared to ordinary visible light, rendering them as cyan.
