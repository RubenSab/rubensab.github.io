Photography

I shoot mainly in infrared (both false-color and black/white) with a modified Lumix FZ18 from 2007 which was "converted" in order to see the "full" spectrum of light (deep blue to near infrared).

This process unlocked a fascinating viewpoint outside our ordinary visible spectrum.

The photos with blue/magenta/red/yellow leaves are taken with a 580 IR pass filter, while the BW and bright pink ones are taken with a 720 IR pass one.
